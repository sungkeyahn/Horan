

Gameplay_SFX


Combo_01 to Combo_10 are ascending sounds of the same sound effect.
They are intended to be used when achieving consecutive combos / results - as many as required can be used...


Points_Add_Loop_** sounds are intended to loop as points are added, followed by the relevant Points_Total_** sound when the total is reached.


Selecting_Loop_** sounds are intended to loop while options are available, (e.g. appearing randomly), followed by the relevant Selected_** sound when selection is made.
